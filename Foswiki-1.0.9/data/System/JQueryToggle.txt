%STARTSECTION{"summary"}%
This is a lightweigted widget to add a toggle feature similar to
the [[Foswiki:Extensions/TwistyPlugin][TwistyPlugin]]. It uses
the means available in jQuery only, i.e. it selectors to toggle
the display of all matching elements.
%ENDSECTION{"summary"}%

---++ Examples

---++ Toggles
%TOGGLE{
  target="#lorem_area" 
  text="Toggle"
  effect="toggle"
}%
%TOGGLE{
  target="#lorem_area"
  text="Fade"
  effect="fade"
}%
%TOGGLE{
  target="#lorem_area"
  text="Slide" 
  effect="slide"
}%
%TOGGLE{
  target="#lorem_area"
  text="Bounce"
  effect="bounce"
}%
%TOGGLE{
  target="#lorem_area"
  text="Ease"
  effect="ease"
}%
%TOGGLE{
  target="#lorem_area"
  text="Elastic"
  effect="elastic"
}%
